Step 6 of 6 — Paint & materials
Paint color
Color match existing paint
Client wants to match their current color exactly
Paint finish
Use this to guide your client. Most condo walls get eggshell. Trim always gets semi-gloss.
FlatCeilings
Zero sheen. Best for ceilings and feature walls. Hides surface imperfections. Not washable.
MatteWalls
Very low sheen. Good for bedrooms and low-traffic rooms. Slightly more washable than flat.
EggshellMost popular
Soft subtle sheen. The go-to for condo and apartment walls. Washable, durable, looks great.
SatinKitchen / bath
Pearl-like sheen. Great for kitchens, bathrooms, and hallways. Easy to wipe clean.
Semi-glossTrim / doors
Noticeable sheen. Standard for all trim, doors, and baseboards. Very durable and washable.
GlossAccents
High shine. Used for accents, furniture, and feature walls. Walls must be very smooth.
